Welcome to Jinbin Hu's Homepage!

I am currently a Post-Doc in the CSE Department of HKUST, advised by Prof. Kai Chen, and a member of intelligent System and networkING Lab (iSING) Lab. I received BS/MS in 2008/2011 from Beijing Jiaotong University and PhD in 2020 from Central South University (Advised by Prof. Jiawei Huang), and joined Changsha University of Science and Technology in December 2021. You can find my CV here.

Research: I work in the areas of datacenter networks and systems. My research aims to improve the performance, robustness, and ease of management of future intelligent network and computing systems. My current research centers on datacenter networking, RDMA networking, AI-centric networking, and machine learning systems.
